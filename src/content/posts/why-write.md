Short version: I hopped on the bandwagon.

Longer version: because thinking out loud is how I figure things out.

There's something that happens when you try to explain a thing you know — you realize exactly how much you don't know about it. Every project I've shipped, every paper I've read, every system I've debugged: the moment I try to write it down clearly, the gaps show up.

So this is a notebook. Not a polished blog with a brand and a posting schedule. Just a place to put things down before I forget them.

If something here is useful to you, that's a bonus.
